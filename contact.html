❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request an anime? +
Use the contact form above with subject "Anime Request" and include the anime name, year, and whether you want Sub or Dub.
Why is a video not playing? +
Try switching to a different server using the server buttons below the player. If all servers fail, please report the issue.
Is GSP ANIME free? +
Yes, GSP ANIME is completely free to use. No registration or subscription required.
How often are episodes updated? +
We aim to index new episodes within hours of their official release. Ongoing series are updated weekly.
