What is GSP ANIME?

GSP ANIME is an anime information and link aggregation website. We index publicly available streaming links from the internet and provide users with a convenient interface to find and access anime content.

We operate similarly to a search engine — we do not store media files, we only provide links to content hosted elsewhere.
